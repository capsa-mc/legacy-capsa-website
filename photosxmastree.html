Christmas Tree Decorations (1997 - Present)

1997: Traditional Chinese decorations with Chinese word for Spring on
the top of the tree
1998: Ornaments are paper cuttings of the 12 animals of the Chinese
Zodiac.  The top of the tree is a shining star.
2000: The ornaments are small lanterns with images of children
at play.  The top of the tree is the Chinese word for Spring.
2001: To commemorate peace, the ornaments are blue or white
pair of doves holding a heart in their beak.  The top of the tree
is a pure angel.
2003: Chinese Lucky Charms hang from the tree like ornaments. 
The top of the tree is the Chinese word for Spring
2007: Winner of the popular vote "Best tree in the Mansion"
2008: Combination of Traditional Christmas decorations and Chinese charms
2009: Combination of Chinese paper cuttings and Chinese charms
