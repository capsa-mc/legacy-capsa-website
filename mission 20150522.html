CAPSA Mission 蒙郡華裔家長學生協會宗旨

  • Present educational issues and needs to the Montgomery County Public Schools (MCPS)
    代表會員向蒙郡公立學校當局表達對教育方針及教學需要的意見
  • Promote cross-cultural understanding in Montgomery County
    向華裔社區家長傳達及解釋蒙郡公立學校的政策及資訊
  • Mobilize and utilize resources to enhance the multi-cultural environment in MCPS and Montgomery County
    幫助會員融入蒙郡社區以增進蒙郡內各族裔間之瞭解
  • Provide assistance to new immigrant families in adjusting their lives to the Montgomery County educational system
    幫助新移民家庭在蒙郡教育制度中適應生活環境
 

CAPSA Task 協會任務

  • Develop materials for members to use when introducing Chinese culture
    彙備及提供中華文化的資料給會員或學校使用
  • Provide volunteers to present the Chinese culture
    幫助義工到學校介紹中華文化
  • Provide volunteers to participate in various MCPS committees
    提供義工參加蒙郡公立學制中各種委員會，以表達華裔社區的意見與需要
  • Provide tutoring program to new immigrants and other needy students
    為華裔學生提供課業輔導
  • Provide Chinese cultural displays. Participate in other cultural activities in the Montgomery County community
    提供義工展示中華文化，及參加多元文化活動
  • Publish newsletters/annual reports to serve as a channel for communication and sharing of ideas amongst members
    出版 “通訊”，作為會員間聯繫，溝通的管道


Tutoring Program Mission 課業輔導宗旨

  • Provide tutoring services to new immigrants and other needy students
    為華裔學生﹑新移民及有需要輔導人士提供課業輔導
  • Share experiences with new immigrant families in Montgomery County in adjusting to the culture
    幫助新移民家庭在蒙郡教育制度中適應生活環境
  • Provide community service and leadership opportunities to qualified high and middle school students
    提供成績優秀的中學生義工服務﹑ 領導才能和教學機會
  • Extend services to adults through English classes
    提供成人英語輔導
  • Present educational issues and needs to the Montgomery County Public Schools (MCPS)
    向蒙郡公立學校當局表達對教育方針及教學需要的意見
