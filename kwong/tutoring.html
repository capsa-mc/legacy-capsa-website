Tutoring Program Information
課業輔導簡介

星期六

Saturday
10:00 AM - 12:00 NOON
Robert Frost Middle School
9201 Scott Drive
Rockville, MD 20850
 Map and Directions to Robert Frost Middle School:  佛洛斯特初中的地圖及往初中的指示 (Click here/請按此欄)

The CAPSA tutoring program has sessions every Saturday from 10 am to 12 noon, between September 8, 2018 and May 18, 2019.  The objective of the CAPSA tutoring program is to provide one-on-one tutoring for needy students in order to provide assistance with their homework and adjusting to the American culture and environment.  The tutoring services are provided by volunteer middle and high school students with good academic standing.  Tutors qualifies for MCPS Student Service Learning (SSL) Hours.  Also, tutors who demonstrate enthusiasm, good leadership and teaching skills may apply to be group coordinators, managing tutoring groups.  Tutoring services are available for members.  Membership is free.  The annual CAPSA shared facility fee is $20 for each tutor or coordinator and $30 for each tutee.  For more information, please contact Dr. Aldrin Leung at (240) 505-6988 or email to capsatutoringprogram@gmail.com.

CAPSA 課業輔導由 2018 年 9 月 8 日至 2019 年 5 月 18 日，逢星期六上午十時至中午十二時在佛洛斯特 (Robert Frost) 初中上課 。課業輔導由成績優秀的中學生擔任義務小老師，採取一對一的方式，協助學生和家長及有需要輔導人士解決課業問題及適應本地的文化與生活。小老師可得到蒙郡公立學校認可的義工服務小時。有工作熱誠，領導才能和教學經驗的小老師們，將可申請擔任輔導協調員。課業輔導提供給年滿五歲以上的會員。不收會員費。小老師/協調員每學年每人場地均攤費 $20 。學生每學年每人場地均攤費 $30 。有意參加接受輔導的學生，及協助義務輔導工作的小老師，請致電梁偉根老師 (240) 505-6988  或 capsatutoringprogram@gmail.com 查詢。
