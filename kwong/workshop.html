Parent Workshops 家長座談會

CAPSA holds Parent Workshops at the Robert Frost Middle School during the school year. Workshops cover a variety of relevant topics focused on education and parenting.

蒙郡華裔家長學生協會每學年在 Robert Frost 初中舉辦家長座談會。主題都是與子女和教育有關的。
