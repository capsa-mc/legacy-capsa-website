Announcements 通告

2007-2008 Tutoring Program Registration

The 2007-2008 CAPSA tutoring program registration will be held from 9:15 to 11:50 am on Saturday, September 8, 2007 at Robert Frost Middle School. Please come on time on the registration date if you are interested in joining CAPSA as either a tutor or tutee. All tutor applicants need to provide a copy of their latest school report card or unofficial transcript during registration, showing their unweighted GPA of at least 3.20 and most recent English grade of B or above.  For more information please contact Aldrin Leung at aldrinleung@gmail.com or (301) 251-9280, (301) 294-2329.

2007-2008 新學年註冊:

2007-2008 課業輔導將訂於九月八日 (星期六) 上午九時十五分至十一時五十分﹐在 Robert Frost 初中辦理新學年註冊。歡迎有意參加小老師工作、接受輔導的學生與家長﹐準時前往 CAPSA 課業輔導註冊。小老師申請者在校成績的Unweighted GPA 必須在3.20 或以上，英文科目必須在B或以上。申請者須攜帶最近期的成績表 (Copy of Report Card or Transcript) 影印本準時前往面試。詳情請致電梁老師 (301) 251-9280, (301) 294-2329 或電郵 aldrinleung@gmail.com 查詢。

Tutoring Program Information
課業輔導簡介

每星期六
上午九時五十分至十一時 五十分

Every Saturday
9:50 AM - 11:50 AM

Robert Frost Middle School
9201 Scott Drive
Rockville, MD 20850

The CAPSA tutoring program has sessions every Saturday from 9:50 am to 11:50 am, between September 8, 2007 and May 17, 2008.  The objective of the CAPSA tutoring program is to provide one-on-one tutoring for needy students and adults in order to provide assistance with their homework and with adjusting to the American culture and environment.  The tutoring services are provided by volunteer middle and high school students in good academic standing.  Tutors qualify for MCPS Student Service Learning (SSL) Hours.  Also, tutors who demonstrate enthusiasm, good leadership and teaching skills may apply for coordinator positions.  We have also extended our services to adults through English classes.  The tutoring service is free for members.  The annual CAPSA membership fee is $20 for individual and $40 for the entire family.  Each adult English class student will share facility fee of $20 for the entire academic year.  Please contact CAPSA if you would like to be either a tutor or a tutee.
