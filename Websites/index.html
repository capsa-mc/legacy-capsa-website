April 10 and 17, 2010 Tutoring and Workshop Facility Change
The CAPSA tutoring program and parent workshop will temporarily be moved to Lakewood Elementary School at 2534 Lindley Terrace, Rockville, MD 20850 on April 10 and 17, 2010 because Robert Frost Middle School is unavailable.

4月10日及 17日課業輔導和家長座談會場地更改。 因校方需用場地，課業輔導和家長座談會將於 4月10日及17日改在 Lakewood 小學舉辦。 地址是 2534 Lindley Terrace, Rockville, MD 20850。

CAPSA tutoring will return to Robert Frost Middle School on April 24, 2010.

4月24日再回到Robert Frost 初中原租場地上 課。

Parent Workshop家長座談會

The CAPSA Parent Workshop will be held on April 17, 2010 from 10 am to 12 noon at Classroom 171 of Lakewood Elementary School. Mrs. Nae Ning Lin from Montgomery County Public Schools will address “How to Nurture a Self-Motivated Learner (Part Two)”. Child care will be provided to school age children during the workshop. All parents, relatives and friends are welcome! For more information, please contact Mrs. Yingju Tsai at (301) 762-5329.

本協會將於四月十七日星期六上午十時至中午十二時在Lakewood 初中171 教室舉辦家長教育座談會。由蒙郡公立學校林余乃寧老師主講。主題為『自發的學習者(二) 』。本會將提供學齡兒童照顧服務，歡迎所有家長和親友們參加這個座談會。詳情請致電簡瑛珠老師 (301) 762-5329查詢。

Founded in 1988, the Chinese American Parents and Students Association (CAPSA) represents its constituents to address educational issues and needs to Montgomery County Public Schools (MCPS) system in Montgomery County, Maryland. The organization also promotes cross-cultural understanding in Montgomery County, provides assistance to new immigrant families in adjusting their lives in the Montgomery County educational system and utilizes a vast resource bank to enhance the multicultural environment in the MCPS.

華裔家長學生協會於1988年成立。本會代表會員向馬里蘭州-蒙哥馬利郡公立學校 (MCPS) 表達對教育方針及教學需要的意見，促進郡內跨文化交流，協助新移民家庭適應蒙哥馬利郡的教育系統，並向蒙哥馬利郡公立學校 (MCPS) 提供資源，加強多元文化教育環境。
